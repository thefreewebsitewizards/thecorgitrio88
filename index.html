Our Story

Meet The Corgi Trio

Hi there! I'm Kara, the proud human of these three amazing Corgis - Opal, Willow Wisp, and Jasper. What started as a simple Instagram account to share photos with family has grown into a joyful community of dog lovers!

Opal (the eldest) is our wise leader with a penchant for snacks. Willow Wisp (the middle child) is our mischievous adventurer. And Jasper (the baby) is our happy-go-lucky ball of energy.

Together, we create content that celebrates the joy of dog ownership, showcases our adventures (both big and small), and hopefully brings a smile to your face every day.
